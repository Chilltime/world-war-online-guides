# Continent View

The Continent View provides a strategic overview of an entire continent, showing all districts, capitals, and general troop movements at a glance.

## What You See

The Continent View displays:

| Element | Description |
|:--------|:------------|
| **Districts** | All districts within the continent |
| **Capitals** | Country capital locations (for Capital Warfare) |
| **Your positions** | Where your troops are stationed |
| **Troop indicators** | General military presence markers |

## Navigation

### Moving Between Continents

From the Continent View, you can:

- **View other continents** - Navigate to different parts of the world
- **Plan movements** - See where your units need to travel
- **Identify targets** - Locate enemy territories and capitals

### Drilling Down

Click on any district to zoom into the [District View](map-district.md) for more detail.

## Strategic Planning

Use the Continent View for:

### Offensive Planning

- **Identify target regions** - Find enemy concentrations
- **Plan multi-district operations** - Coordinate attacks across areas
- **Locate capitals** - Find Capital Warfare objectives

### Defensive Planning

- **Monitor borders** - Watch for enemy movements toward your territory
- **Position reserves** - Place units to reinforce multiple districts
- **Protect key assets** - Ensure coverage of important bases

## Continents in World War Online

The game world consists of multiple continents, each containing:

- Multiple countries
- Numerous districts per country
- Capital cities for competitive warfare
- Varied terrain (guerrilla and coastal districts)
