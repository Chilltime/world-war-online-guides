# Battle Statistics

Battle Statistics track your combat performance in World War Online, providing insights into your victories, defeats, and overall effectiveness as a commander.

## Statistics Tracked

Your battle statistics include:

| Statistic | Description |
|:----------|:------------|
| **Total Battles** | Number of battles you've participated in |
| **Victories** | Battles won (Decisive Victory) |
| **Defeats** | Battles lost |
| **Win Rate** | Percentage of battles won |
| **Bases Conquered** | Total bases taken with Generals |
| **Bases Destroyed** | Total bases destroyed with Special Forces |
| **Capitals Captured** | Capital bases conquered during warfare |

## Understanding Win Rate

Your win rate is calculated as:

```
Win Rate = (Victories / Total Battles) Ã— 100
```

A higher win rate indicates:

- Better unit composition choices
- Effective tactical planning
- Good target selection

## Improving Your Statistics

### Attack Strategy

- **Scout before attacking** - Check enemy defenses in districts
- **Match composition** - Counter enemy unit types effectively
- **Use appropriate force** - Don't over-commit or under-commit units

### Defense Strategy

- **Set up defenses proactively** - Don't leave bases undefended
- **Use dynamic strategies** - Ambush and Flank can turn battles
- **Layer defenses** - Combine static and dynamic positioning

### Target Selection

- **Choose winnable battles** - Don't attack obviously superior forces
- **Assess risk vs reward** - Consider what you gain from victory
- **Time your attacks** - Coordinate with squad operations

## Statistics and Leaderboards

Your battle statistics contribute to:

- **Player Leaderboard** - Individual ranking based on battle wins
- **Personal reputation** - Other players can see your combat record
- **Squad recruitment** - Active squads look for players with good stats
