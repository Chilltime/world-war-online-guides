# Store Sections

![Available Supply Crates](../assets/images/store_crates.webp "Supply Crates")

The Store contains several sections beyond pack purchases, including free Supply Crates, Special Deals, and collectible items.

## Supply Crates

Supply Crates provide free rewards based on your gameplay activity. Collect them regularly to maximize your earnings.

### Crate Types

| Crate | Based On | Collection Interval |
|:------|:---------|:-------------------:|
| **Experience Crate** | Experience earned | Every 8 hours |
| **Damage Crate** | Damage dealt in attacks | Every 12 hours |
| **Supreme Crate** | Supreme units killed in battles | Every 24 hours |
| **Capital Crate** | Capital points earned for battalion | Every 24 hours |

### Maximizing Crate Rewards

- **Play actively** to generate higher crate values
- **Collect on time** - Don't let crates sit uncollected
- **Participate in warfare** - Capital Crates reward battalion activity
- **Use Supreme Units in battle** - Supreme Crates reward combat against elite units

## Daily WWO-Influence

The Store provides free WWO-Influence daily:

| Feature | Details |
|:--------|:--------|
| **Daily Collection** | Free WWO-Influence available |
| **Maximum** | Up to 2,000 WWO per collection period |
| **No Purchase Required** | Available to all players |

Visit the Store regularly to claim your free premium currency.

## Special Deals

Special Deals offer discounted packages of units and experience:

### What's Included

- **Unit packs** at reduced WWO-Influence costs
- **Experience bonuses** bundled with units
- **Limited-time offers** that rotate periodically

### Value Assessment

Compare Special Deals to standard training costs:

- Calculate the WWO cost per unit
- Factor in experience value
- Consider your current needs

## First Purchase Season Pack

Your first purchase each season unlocks bonus Supreme Units:

| Trigger | Reward |
|:--------|:-------|
| First pack purchase of the season | 50% of pack value in Supreme Units |
| Collectible in Store | Appears as unit packs |

This bonus resets each season, encouraging at least one purchase per competitive cycle.

## Coupon Codes

The Store accepts promotional coupon codes:

- **Official promotions** from World War Online
- **Event rewards** from special occasions
- **Redemption** through the Store interface

Watch official channels for code announcements.
