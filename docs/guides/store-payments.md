# Payment Methods

World War Online accepts various payment methods for purchasing WWO-Influence packs through the Store.

## Supported Payment Methods

The Store supports multiple payment options to accommodate players worldwide:

| Method | Availability |
|:-------|:-------------|
| **Credit/Debit Cards** | Visa, MasterCard, American Express |
| **Digital Wallets** | PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay |
| **Mobile Payments** | Carrier billing (where available) |
| **Regional Options** | Varies by country |

## Making a Purchase

### Step-by-Step Process

1. **Open the Store** from the main menu
2. **Select a pack** from available options
3. **Choose payment method** from available options
4. **Confirm purchase** and complete payment
5. **Receive WWO-Influence** immediately in your account

### Purchase Limits

Remember the weekly purchase limits:

| Limit | Details |
|:------|:--------|
| **Maximum** | 10,000 WWO worth per 6-day period |
| **Interface** | Shows remaining allowance |
| **Countdown** | Displays when limit resets |

## Security

### Safe Transactions

- All payments processed through secure channels
- No payment information stored in-game
- Transaction confirmations sent to your email

### Purchase Protection

- Review purchases carefully before confirming
- Contact support for transaction issues
- Keep records of purchase confirmations

## Regional Considerations

### Currency

- Prices displayed in your local currency (where supported)
- Exchange rates applied at time of purchase
- Final price shown before confirmation

### Availability

- Some payment methods vary by region
- Check available options in your Store
- Alternative methods provided if primary unavailable

## Refund Policy

For information about refunds and purchase disputes:

- Review the [Store Rules](../legal/store-rules.md)
- Contact support for specific issues
- Understand the terms before purchasing

## Tips for Purchasing

### Plan Your Purchases

- Consider the weekly limit when planning
- Time purchases for when you need WWO most
- Take advantage of First Purchase Season bonuses

### Maximize Value

- Compare pack values before buying
- Consider battalion benefits from bonus packs
- Watch for Special Deals in the Store

### Budget Responsibly

- Set personal spending limits
- The game is enjoyable without purchases
- Premium currency provides advantages but isn't required
